Who I Am

Extreme
Thinking.

I'm Frank Ricci. For over ten years, I've been designing vehicle wraps for clients across Europe — from street cars to showcase builds, from race liveries to full fleet graphics.

What I do is different from what you see everywhere else. I don't apply vinyl. I design what goes on it. Every project starts with a method: study the vehicle, read the reference, break it apart, build something new.

Today, Frank Ricci Design is more than a studio. It's a design methodology, a learning platform, and a growing ecosystem for anyone serious about automotive wrap design.
